About SWIM@KSoM

The Summer Workshop in Mathematics at Kerala School of Mathematics (SWIM@KSoM) is a rigorous and focused instructional program designed to provide a deep dive into fundamental mathematical areas. The workshop typically features in-depth lectures covering key concepts in Mathematical Analysis, Abstract Algebra, and Linear Algebra.

To reinforce understanding and develop problem-solving skills, these lectures are complemented by dedicated problem-solving sessions. This intensive program is specifically tailored for highly motivated students, usually in their final year of a Bachelor of Science degree or early years of a Master's program. Participants benefit from instruction delivered by experienced and highly regarded mathematicians in their respective fields.
